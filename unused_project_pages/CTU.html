CTU Map

Cartographic Labeling Exercise

The CTU Map represents the City, Township and Unorganized Territories of Minnesota. This task presented during an ArcGIS course promoted applying cartographic labeling conventions as well as learning ArcGIS for Desktop's cartography tools such as Maplex Label Engine. In addition to label placement, my group decdied to experiment with typography as well as color.

Software: ArcGIS for Desktop, Maplex Label Engine
