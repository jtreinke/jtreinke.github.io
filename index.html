About
__________

Hello. My name is Jennifer, part this, part that, based in Minneapolis. I develop front-end projects at Carmichael Lynch. I use my multidisciplinary background in geographic information systems, programming, and design to solve problems.

My focus is creating visual narratives to convey complex information to a broad audience. I love the intersection of data and design because there is a beauty in the way underlying elements like psychology, culture, and space intertwine enabling information exchange.

In my freetime, I’m a wannabe polyglot who loves a garden party. I value the innovation that comes through collaboration with others. If you’d like to connect with me on a project, to get in touch on LinkedIn, take a glimpse at my GitHub.
