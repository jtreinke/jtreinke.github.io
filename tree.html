City of Lakeville: Park Tree Inventories

Emerald Ash Borer Risk Assessment



As the environmental resources intern for the City of Lakeville, I performed a tree inventory of the city’s parks. The tree inventory was necessary for a cost – analysis report for when/if the Emerald Ash Borer hit the city. I had to input the tree coordinates in a GPS with several domains consisting of tree identification, circumference, and condition. I had to create maps using the tree inventory information in addition to satellite imagery from Dakota County for City Council Meetings. The map represents one of parks assessed.

Software used: Trimble GPS, Microsoft Excel, Cartegraph, and ArcGIS
