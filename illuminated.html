Illuminated Contours

Hand Drawn Map Project

During my undergraduate cartography course, I was inspired to do a hand drawn Tanaka Method or Illuminated Contours map. I chose a small segment of northern California for varied relief. The Illuminated Contours map is a replica of a USGS topological map of this area. I adopted the sunlight direction from the northwest and used graphite pencil to complete the work.

Software: USGS Topological Map, pencil
