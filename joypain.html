Mapping of Joy & Pain

Concept Development and Landing Page

For the Mapping of Joy and Pain project, I assisted in the initial concept formulation and development as well as creating a responsive landing page. The project originated from University of Minnesota artist and faculty member Rebecca Krinke as a form of public art. A laser cut wood engraving of Minneapolis and St. Paul placed in communal areas, attracted people to share through drawing on the map, areas where they felt joy and/or pain. We converted her work of art to a web map as to continue portraying the complexity of emotional space.
