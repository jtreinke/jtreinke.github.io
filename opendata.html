Open Data Tracking

Minnesota County GIS Data Availability



As part of my graduate assistantship, Open Data Tracking is a project where I surveyed each county in Minnesota through their official website. I recorded what types of GIS data they provided (e.g. parcels and centerlines), if the data was available for a cost or given gratuitously, and if metadata was present. I compiled the characteristics of the counties and mapped the data using CartoDB. Click here to see full map

Software Used: Excel, CartoDB
