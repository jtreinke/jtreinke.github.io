Cicatrix Beauty

An Online Archive of Scars

While exploring web design, I created an online archive featuring scars. I chose to photograph the scars of friends and family and document the stories bound to these markers of time. The website aims to uncover the impact of scars on the individuals that bare them. Scars relate memories fluctuating from comical to tragic events, and acts as a connecting force.
