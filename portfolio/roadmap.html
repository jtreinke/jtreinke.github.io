Arrowhead Road Map

Reference of Northeastern Minnesota

As part of my ArcGIS course, I practiced cartography and managing an abundance of data by designing a road map for the Arrowhead region in northeastern Minnesota. The map featured major highways, and national and state parks, in addition to camping sites and wilderness areas.

Software: ArcGIS and Adobe Photoshop
