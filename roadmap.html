Arrowhead Road Map

Generated reference map for navigation



As part of my ArcGIS course, I practiced cartography and managing an abundance of data by designing a road map for the Arrowhead region in northeastern part of Minnesota. The map featured major highways, and national and state parks, in addition camping sites and wilderness areas.

Software used: ArcGIS and Adobe Photoshop
