Minneapolis Smell Map

Pulling Scents from the Twittersphere

The Minneapolis Smell Map portrays scents detected around the Twin Cities based on tweets from November 15, 2015 - December 15, 2015. I was fascinated by the projects like the Smell Map of London where a group of researchers sought to discover the types of smells that composed the city. I was curious to see if Minneapolis had similar results and thus learned the methods for pulling tweets from Twitter. I tested several platforms such as Tweepy, a Python Library, and TAGS, a Google Sheet template, in addition to a compilation of aromatic defintions used from research studies to call hashtags.
